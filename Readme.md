🌀 Jutsu Sim: Rasengan vs. Chidori
An interactive, AI-powered augmented reality experience that lets you channel your inner shinobi. Using MediaPipe Hands, this project detects your hand gestures in real-time to summon iconic elemental powers—Naruto's Rasengan and Sasuke's Chidori.

🌟 Features
Dual Hand Recognition: * Left Hand: Summons the swirling Rasengan.

Right Hand: Unleashes the crackling Chidori.

Gestural Activation: The powers only manifest when you open your palms. Closing your hand causes the energy to dissipate.

Dynamic Tracking: The effects are pinned to your palms and knuckles, moving fluidly with your hand movements.

Neon Skeleton UI: Overlays a bright blue, glowing digital skeleton over your hands for a high-tech "shinobi" aesthetic.

🚀 How to Use
Assets Setup: Ensure you have an assets folder containing:

naruto.mp4 (The Rasengan effect)

sasuke.mp4 (The Chidori effect)

Launch: Open the HTML file in a modern web browser.

Permissions: Allow camera access when prompted.

Channel Your Chakra: * Show your Left Palm to the camera to summon the Rasengan.

Show your Right Palm to summon the Chidori.

Control: Close your hand into a fist to stop the technique.

🎨 Customization
Sensitivity: You can adjust minDetectionConfidence in the script to make the hand tracking more or less strict.

Visuals: The darkness div and mix-blend-mode: screen are used to make the video assets transparent and glowing. You can swap the .mp4 files for any other elemental effect (like fire or water) to change your "Nature Transformation."

Size: Adjust the #n and #s widths in the CSS to change the scale of the jutsu.
